## The Power of  Generative AI

Generative AI, also  known as generative adversarial networks (GANs) is  a subset of artificial intelligence that focuses on creating new content. Unlike traditional AI models that rely  on pre-existing data, generative AI has  the ability to generate original and unique  output. It works  by pitting two neural networks  against each other - a generator network and a discriminator network. The generator network creates new content, while the discriminator network evaluates the authenticity of that content. Through this iterative process, generative AI learns and improves, producing increasingly realistic and innovative results.

## Rapid Prototyping: From Idea to Reality

Rapid  prototyping is a crucial step in the product  development process. It allows designers and engineers to quickly iterate on their ideas test concepts and gather feedback. Traditionally, prototyping involved time-consuming manual processes but  with generative  AI on the cloud, the game has changed.

Generative AI enables designers to explore countless design possibilities in a  fraction of the time. By inputting a  set of design parameters,  such  as size, shape, and material generative AI algorithms can generate a multitude of design options. These options can then be further refined and optimized resulting in highly efficient and  innovative prototypes.
